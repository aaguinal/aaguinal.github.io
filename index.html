Angeline Aguinaldo


Github: https://github.com/aaguinal
Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=7-4RZdcAAAAJ
Email: aaguinal@cs.umd.edu

I recently defended my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park [defense slides]. I am advised by William Regli [bio]. My research explores formal approaches based on category theory to tackle problems in knowledge-based task planning, plan transfer, and plan composition for AI and robotics applications.

I am also a research staff member at the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory, where I have developed software platforms for geospatial computer vision processing in support of humanitarian and disaster relief efforts [ref], as well as for testing and evaluating metagenomic classifiers [ref].

My research areas of interest include AI planning, robotics, knowledge representation, and category theory. My hobbies include musical theater and video editing.
